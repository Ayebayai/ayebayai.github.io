WAJU

Waju(Nigerian singer, composer, songwriter, performance and recording artist) who loves to work with different music style as a fusion artist of jazz, afrobeat, Rock and African flok. Obanla Japheth Olanrewaju was born in the year 2002 on the 2nd of August. He is from Ondo State, He attented Nigerian Navy Secondary School for the secondary education. He started his music career in the year 2018 with songwriting for different artists and started recording his own song in 2020. He met with a producer Nuel who shed more light on his music journey by putting him through some process and from there Waju started producing his songs himself till date! In December 22,2022 Waju drooped an Album titled EKUNDAYO., Which was recorded,mixed and mastered by Waju He spoke further about the album explaining the inspirations behind the album he said, He was inspired by the love he has for folk and story telling which he got from artists like Fela Kuti,Brymo,Lagbaja,King Sunny Ade and the likes....

EKUNDAYO

EKUNDAYO is an album of 8 songs that straighly touches some untouched topics of the society. Waju wrote of the songs in question of why those topics are not being touched and finally he answered them in the sound,He fusioned the sounds with jazz,folk,pop, & RnB. PULCHRA OPUS

PULCHRA OPUS The second studio album, is a fusion of Sounds and stories and phases. from the Title PULCHRA OPUS which means Beautiful Art in Latin, gives an insight on how beautiful the album is. The Album consists of 8 tracks and it was all produced,mixed & mastered by Waju.The Album is out on all Digital platforms such as Apple music,Spotify,Boomplay,Deezer……..
